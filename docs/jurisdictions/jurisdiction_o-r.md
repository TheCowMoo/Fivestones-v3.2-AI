# Workplace Violence Liability: O-R States Guide

**States Covered:** Oklahoma, Oregon
**Last Updated:** May 11, 2026

---

## Oklahoma

**Workplace Violence Prevention Requirements:**
- No comprehensive statewide workplace violence law
- Healthcare facilities have some requirements
- Focus on energy sector safety
- Limited state-level enforcement

**Key Citations:**
- Oklahoma Statutes Title 40 § 401 et seq. (Oklahoma Occupational Safety and Health Act)
- Oklahoma Administrative Code 380:1-1 et seq.
- OSHA General Duty Clause

**Industry Focus:** Energy, healthcare, manufacturing

---

## Oregon

**Workplace Violence Prevention Requirements:**
- No comprehensive statewide law
- Healthcare facilities regulated under Oregon OSHA
- Some local requirements in Portland Metro area
- Focus on emergency preparedness and reporting

**Key Citations:**
- ORS 654.010-654.995 (Oregon Safe Employment Act)
- OAR 437-002 (Oregon OSHA Rules)
- Portland City Code Chapter 3.102 (Workplace Safety)

**Industry Focus:** Healthcare, education, public safety

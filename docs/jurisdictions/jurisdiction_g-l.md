# Workplace Violence Liability: G-L States Guide

**States Covered:** Georgia, Hawaii, Louisiana
**Last Updated:** May 11, 2026

---

## Georgia

**Workplace Violence Prevention Requirements:**
- No comprehensive statewide workplace violence law
- Healthcare facilities have enhanced requirements
- Atlanta metropolitan area has local ordinances
- Focus on emergency response planning

**Key Citations:**
- Georgia Code § 34-2A-1 et seq. (Georgia Occupational Safety and Health Act)
- Georgia Rules and Regulations 300-2-1 et seq.
- OSHA General Duty Clause

**Industry Focus:** Healthcare, education, retail

---

## Hawaii

**Workplace Violence Prevention Requirements:**
- No specific statewide workplace violence law
- Island geography affects emergency response
- Focus on tourism and healthcare sectors
- Some local ordinances in Honolulu

**Key Citations:**
- Hawaii Revised Statutes § 396-1 et seq. (Hawaii Occupational Safety and Health Act)
- Hawaii Administrative Rules § 12-46
- Honolulu Municipal Code

**Industry Focus:** Tourism, healthcare, agriculture

---

## Louisiana

**Workplace Violence Prevention Requirements:**
- No specific statewide workplace violence prevention law
- Healthcare facilities regulated under Louisiana OSHA
- Focus on petrochemical industry safety
- Some local requirements in Baton Rouge/New Orleans

**Key Citations:**
- Louisiana Revised Statutes 23:1291 et seq. (Louisiana Occupational Safety and Health Act)
- Louisiana Administrative Code 46:I.101 et seq.
- OSHA General Duty Clause

**Industry Focus:** Petrochemical, healthcare, maritime
